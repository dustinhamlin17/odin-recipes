Jalapeno Popper Recipe

Description

This amazing Jalapeno Popper recipe will have your mouth watering at the sight of it

This quick and simple recipe will surely be a huge hit with the family, assuming they aren't afraid of a little spice from a stray seed or two.

Ingredients

  • 12 jalapeno peppers
  • 6 ounces cream cheese (softened)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 4 ounces sharp chedder (shredded)
  • 2 tablespoons chives (finely chopped)
  • 1/4 cup Panko bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon butter (melted)
  • 24 strips of bacon (two per pepper)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees
  2. Wearing gloves, slice the jalapenos in half lengthwise. Using a small spoon scoop out the seeds and membranes.
  3. In a bowl mix cream cheese, garlic powder, chedder cheese and chives (if using)
  4. In a separate bowl, combine Panko crumbs and melted butter.
  5. Fill jalapenos with the cheese mixture. Top with crumbs.
  6. Wrap each popper with two strips of bacon.
  7. Place on a baking pan and bake 18-22 minutes or until golden.
  8. Cool 5-10 minutes before serving.
