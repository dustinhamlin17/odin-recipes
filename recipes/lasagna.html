Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is the ultimate lasagna recipe. Handed down by generations of italian mothers. This is one for the books.

This recipe will take approximately 2 hours to complete and will give you enough to serve 6 people. Make sure to prep ahead of time.

Ingredients

  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Spices
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Beef
  • Sausage
  • Mozzarella
  • Lasagna
  • Egg
  • Parsley
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce
  3. Combine cheese mixture
  4. Layer & Bake
